Traveling in Europe ✈

Italy➡Spain➡Belgium➡France➡Netherlands

The trip began from the United States where I relocated to Naples, Italy. I had taken a few trips from Italy to Belgium as well as Barcelona, Spain. I enjoyed the festivals of Tomorrowland while in Belgium. After a little over 2 years in Italy, my time there was coming to a close I decided to take another trip to Belgium before going back to the United States. During the first trip, I decided to hang around for at least the next few months. The trip included sights of France, the Netherlands, and places in Belgium. All of Europe enticed me; this made me decide to take on the process of my dual citizenship from the Czech Republic. I have loved traveling in Europe.
